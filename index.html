How did you do that?!

Well, that's how I felt in the beginning! Don't worry, it is easy even if you don't have any background knowledge in computer science (like me). I started my venture in CodeHS, where I took up the first free course in programming the "Introduction to programming with Karel the Dog". And believe me it was initially a bit hard (especially the typing), but when you get used to it is really fun and interesting.

What's that?

CodeHS is a programming made simple website which has devised a curriculum to make the seemingly boring coding into an understandable, fun and enjoyable curriculum using a dog named Karel. There are multiple curriculums, courses and more available for teachers and students likewise, some free of cost and some with a cost. Many teachers and students have found CodeHS very helpful.
